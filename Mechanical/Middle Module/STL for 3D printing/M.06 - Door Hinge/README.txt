# Summary

Simple hinge I designed to work on a 3D printer enclosure. It's designed for M3 hardware. 

The holes are 30mm apart, and it is 18mm from the center line of the holes to the center line of the hinge barrel.

Print standing up to keep hinge features as round as possible.
